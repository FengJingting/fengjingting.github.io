Introduction

Nice to meet you! I am Feng Jingting, a third year student in University of Leeds and South-West Jiaotong University. I built this website because various interesting ideas pops out from my mind when I was doing research and projects. I want to record them down and inspire myself to go further. What’s more, I also want to use this website as place to record my learning experience, difficulties I have encountered. If you find it is interesting or useful for you, you can star my website!

About My Research Area

Now

I am very interested in robot motion planning and I serve as a algorithm developer in our school’s RoboMaster team now. I am responsible for the motion planning of our automatic sentry. I will update its image if my mechanical team member successfully fixed it. But you can catch a glimpse of such interesting stuff in the following picture:

Well, as sentry is the only robots that is totally automatic, I should design what it should do before the real combat begin. At first, I tried to use reinforcement learning and simulation in Unity to help it make decision, but unfortunately, it is time-consuming and resources- consuming. I temporarily quit this idea because of limited time. If you are interested in this area, you can see this article: XXX.

Now, my strategy is using behaviour tree to help it make decision. Thus, I choose Behaviourtree.cpp( Introduction to BTs | BehaviorTree.CPP ) , a open source library related to ROS. And I want to complete at least 6 behaviours in the following three weeks:

Behaviour Description
Partol At the beginning of competition, patrolling around base to watch the enemy.
Escape Comprehensive consideration in combination with number, position, life status of enemy and teammates
Shoot Comprehensive consideration in combination with number, position, life status of enemy and teammates
Go Home Navigate back to home
Chase Comprehensive consideration in combination with number, position, life status of enemy and teammates
Corporate Have no idea now
